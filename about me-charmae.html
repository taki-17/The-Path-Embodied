Why I Chose 'The Nowhere Safe' Hirono Figurine

I chose the character Nowhere Safe because she reminds us that sometimes, the hardest part of life isn’t the journey itself—it’s not knowing where you truly belong. I see myself in this character because there are times when I feel lost. Even when I’m surrounded by friends or family, I sometimes feel like I’m not in the right place. It’s not that I don’t feel safe with them; it’s just that there are moments when I feel the need to be alone, because deep down, I feel like I don’t belong. I feel disconnected—even from the people I’ve grown used to. This makes my journey in life more difficult, because I often feel empty and not enough. I constantly feel the pressure to become more—perfect at something—just to feel like I truly belong in the group I’m with.
